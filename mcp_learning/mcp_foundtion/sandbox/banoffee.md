# Banoffee Pie

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
- 1/2 cup butter, melted
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3 bananas, sliced 1/4 inch thick
- 1 cup dulce de leche
- 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
- 1 1/2 teaspoons instant espresso powder
- 3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Chocolate shavings (optional)

## Directions

### Step 1: Make the graham cracker crust
Preheat the oven to 3506F. In a large bowl, stir together the graham cracker crumbs, butter, sugar, and salt.

### Step 2: Form and bake the crust
Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch deep-dish pie plate. Bake the crust until set, about 15 minutes. Cool the crust completely to room temperature.

### Step 3: Whip up the topping
Place the heavy whipping cream, espresso powder, confectioners sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl. Whisk by hand or with beaters until soft peaks form. Set aside.

### Step 4: Add the filling
Spread the dulce de leche into the cooled crust using a spatula.

### Step 5: Spread on the whipped cream
Top the dulce de leche with two layers of sliced bananas. Spoon the whipped cream over the bananas.

### Step 6: Decorate the pie
Refrigerate the banoffee pie for at least three hours or overnight. Garnish with chocolate shavings before serving if desired.

## Tips
- Use firm but ripe bananas for best flavor and texture.
- To prevent bananas from browning, cut them just before assembling or squeeze fresh lemon juice on them.
- For easier spreading of dulce de leche, dip your spatula in hot water before use.

Enjoy this delicious and easy-to-make classic British dessert!
